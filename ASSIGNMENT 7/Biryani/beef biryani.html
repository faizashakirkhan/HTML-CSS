BEEF BIRYANI

Beef Biryani is a rice-based dish made with spices, rice (usually basmati) and beef, mutton, fish, eggs, or vegetables. The name is derived from the Persian word berya(n), which means "fried" or "roasted". Alternative spellings include biriani, biriyani, buriyani, beryani or beriani.
